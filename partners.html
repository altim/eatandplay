New York

New York City. The Big Apple. Gotham. The City that Never Sleeps. No matter what you call it, now you can have it all right in the palm of your hand. New York can be expensive with dining often a visitor’s single biggest expense – and there’s no better or easier way to save in New York than Eat and Play Card.

Eat and Play Card has joined forces with Gray Line New York to create the city’s most complete and only savings program that covers just about everything. Nobody knows vacation savings better than Eat and Play Card and nobody knows New York better than Gray Line New York. Together we’ve brought you exclusive savings and sweet deals at some of Manhattan’s best and most popular restaurants, attractions, shops and entertainment – nothing will save you more. Whether you’re in Midtown, Uptown, Downtown, Greenwich, Chelsea, Little Italy, the Garment District or many other Manhattan neighborhoods, you’ll find great savings at places you love, with Eat and Play Card.
